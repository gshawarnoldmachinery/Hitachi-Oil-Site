Hitachi Genuine Coolant

Reference for Hitachi Long-Life Coolant mixed 30 to 50 percent

Product: Hitachi Genuine Long-Life Coolant (LLC)

Coolant Type
Use Hitachi Construction Machinery Genuine Long-Life Coolant. The listed alternative is an organic type corrosion inhibitor long life coolant.
Mix Ratio
Use genuine Hitachi Long-Life Coolant mixed by 30 to 50 percent. If coolant mixed with less than 30 percent Hitachi Long-Life Coolant is used, freezing damage or corrosion may shorten cooling system life.
Freeze Protection
Ready-to-use Hitachi coolant is shown with freeze protection to -37 C / -35 F. Maintaining at least 30 percent Hitachi Long-Life Coolant helps protect against freezing damage and supports proper protection of cooling system parts.
Formulation
Proper coolant concentration also helps control corrosion and maintain stable cooling system performance under load. The formulation is free from silicates, phosphates, chromates, nitrites and boron.

Maintenance Do / Don't

Do
  • Use Hitachi Construction Machinery Genuine Long-Life Coolant when servicing the cooling system.
  • Maintain a coolant concentration between 30 and 50 percent.
  • Check coolant level and reservoir condition during routine inspections.
  • Keep radiator fins and cooling package clean for effective airflow.
Don't
  • Do not use coolant mixed at less than 30 percent Hitachi Long-Life Coolant.
  • Do not mix unknown coolant chemistries without compatibility guidance.
  • Do not use contaminated or hard water when mixing concentrate.
  • Do not run with low coolant level or unresolved leaks.
Important Warning

Do not use coolant that does not meet the required concentration or product type requirements.

  • Use genuine Hitachi Long-Life Coolant mixed by 30 to 50 percent.
  • Coolant mixed with less than 30 percent Hitachi Long-Life Coolant may shorten cooling system life through freezing damage or corrosion.
  • If using an alternative product, use organic type corrosion inhibitor long life coolant.
