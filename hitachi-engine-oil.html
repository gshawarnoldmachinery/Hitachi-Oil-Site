Maintenance Do / Don't

Do
  • Use Hitachi Construction Machinery Genuine Engine Oil 10W-40 DH-2 whenever possible.
  • If using an alternative product, make sure it conforms to JASO DH-2.
  • Check oil level daily and top up with the same oil type.
  • Use the oil within the stated air temperature range of -20 to 40 C (-4 to 104 F).
Don't
  • Do not use engine oil that does not conform to the required specification.
  • Do not use unsuitable oil, because engine and aftertreatment device damage or performance deterioration may result.
  • Do not use unsuitable oil if warranty coverage is a concern, because resulting engine damage may be excluded from Hitachi warranty policy.
  • Do not mix unknown oils without compatibility checks.
Important Warning

Do not use engine oil that does not meet the required specification or requirements.

  • If not using Hitachi Construction Machinery Genuine Engine Oil, use engine oil conforming to JASO DH-2.
  • Unsuitable oil may damage the engine or aftertreatment device, or reduce performance.
  • Damage caused by unsuitable oil may be excluded from Hitachi warranty policy.
