Maintenance Do / Don't

Do
  • Use Hitachi Construction Machinery Genuine Hydraulic Oil Super 46HN whenever possible.
  • Use zinc-free hydraulic oil, because Hitachi requires zinc-free hydraulic oil for its machines.
  • If using an alternative product, make sure it conforms to JCMAS HK VG46W.
  • Keep reservoirs, breathers, and fill points clean during service.
  • Monitor hydraulic oil level and condition, with a normal oil change interval of 4000 hours.
Don't
  • Do not use oil that does not meet the specified requirements.
  • Do not assume the oil should always run to 5000 hours if condition shows earlier degradation.
  • Do not use unsuitable oil, because machine damage may result and may be excluded from Hitachi warranty policy.
  • Do not mix unknown hydraulic oils without compatibility guidance.
Important Warning

Do not use hydraulic oil that does not meet the required specification or requirements.

  • Hitachi requires zinc-free hydraulic oil. Genuine Hydraulic Oil Super 46HN is zinc-free.
  • If not using genuine hydraulic oil, use hydraulic oil conforming to JCMAS HK VG46W.
  • Unsuitable oil may cause machine damage and may be excluded from Hitachi warranty policy.
