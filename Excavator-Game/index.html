How To Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move and dig.
  • Press R to place dirt ramps back to surface.
  • You start above ground at surface level.
  • Upgrade from ZX17 up to ZX890LCH.
  • Each machine has its own depth and ore limits.
  • Watch for power lines, water lines, collapses, and lava pockets.

Ore Access

Gold/Emerald: large mining class

Diamond: only the biggest class

Risk Zones

Top layers: possible utility water and power lines

Mid depth: water pockets and collapse risk

Deep layers: lava pockets and high fall danger
