Maintenance Do / Don't

Do
  • Use GL-4 90 gear oil where the machine specifies this gear oil grade.
  • Use clean oil that protects gears under heavy load.
  • Keep housings, breathers, and fill points clean during service.
  • Monitor oil condition during scheduled gear service.
  • Follow the machine service manual and dealer guidance for maintenance intervals.
Don't
  • Do not use oil that does not match the recommended GL-4 and SAE 90 grades.
  • Do not ignore signs of degraded gear oil or contamination.
  • Do not run with unsuitable oil in gear applications.
  • Do not assume a non-matching oil will protect gears and metal surfaces the same way.
Important Warning

Use the gear oil recommended by Hitachi Construction Machinery for the gear application.

  • Use the recommended GL-4 90 gear oil for the specified gear application.
  • Using unsuitable oil may reduce wear protection, corrosion protection, and gear durability.
  • Do not use oils that do not meet the required recommendation or specification.
