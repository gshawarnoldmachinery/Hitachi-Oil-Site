Maintenance Do / Don't

Do
  • Use Hitachi axle oil where the machine specifies axle oil for wheel loader axle service.
  • Keep axle housings and fill points clean during service.
  • Monitor oil condition during axle maintenance intervals.
  • Use oil intended for wet disc brakes and limited slip or torque proportional differential service.
  • Follow the machine service manual and dealer guidance for maintenance intervals.
Don't
  • Do not use unsuitable oil in wheel loader axle applications.
  • Do not ignore signs of degraded axle oil or contamination.
  • Do not run with oil that does not protect wet brakes, gears, and bearings.
  • Do not assume a non-matching oil will control wet brake judder noise the same way.
Important Warning

Do not use axle oil that does not meet the required specification or requirements.

  • Hitachi Construction Machinery Genuine Axle Oils are specially designed and tested to provide optimum performance for Hitachi Construction Machinery.
  • Hitachi recommends using Genuine Axle Oil. Using other oil may lead to machine damage.
  • Do not use oils that do not meet the required specification or requirements.
  • Unsuitable oil may cause machine damage and may be excluded from Hitachi warranty policy.
