Recommended Transmission Oil

10W
GRADE
10W Grade
10W is the transmission oil viscosity grade. It identifies the oil thickness level used for this Hitachi transmission oil reference and is recommended for ambient temperatures of minus 25 C or higher.

Quick Legend

10W = transmission oil viscosity grade.
Temperature = recommended for ambient conditions of -25 C or higher.
Application = transmission.

Performance Focus

Thermal Stability

Oxidation Control
Refined base oil and antioxidants maintain viscosity and resist sludge formation under high load.

Maintenance Do / Don't

Do
  • Use Hitachi Construction Machinery Transmission Oil 10W where the machine specifies this transmission oil.
  • Use the 10W transmission oil recommended for the transmission application.
  • Use clean oil that supports smooth shifting and low-temperature transmission operation.
  • Keep the transmission system clean and monitor fluid condition during service.
  • Follow the machine service manual and dealer guidance for maintenance intervals.
Don't
  • Do not use oil that does not match the recommended grade and class.
  • Do not ignore signs of degraded transmission oil or contamination.
  • Do not run with unsuitable oil in the transmission application.
  • Do not assume a non-matching oil will protect transmission components the same way.
Important Warning

Use the transmission oil recommended by Hitachi Construction Machinery for the transmission application.

  • Use Hitachi Construction Machinery Transmission Oil 10W for the transmission application.
  • Using unsuitable oil may reduce shift quality, corrosion protection, and drivetrain durability.
  • Unsuitable oil may cause machine damage and may be excluded from Hitachi warranty policy.
  • Do not use oils that do not meet the required recommendation or specification.
